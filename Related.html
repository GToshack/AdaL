Important to Ada's Story

Charles Babbage

Charles Babbage was a professor and a mathematician. He developped the Difference engine and later the Analytical Engine, the one used by Ada. They became great friends after first being introduced, writing many letters.

Mary Somerville

Mary Somervlle was a british scientific writer who authored books on topics such as physics, astronomy, and geography. She tutored Ada when Ada was young, and later introduced her to many scientists including Charles Babbage.
